Alaska

"ə-LASS-kə"

Alaska, the largest and northernmost state in the U.S., is known for its stunning natural beauty and vast wilderness. With rugged mountain ranges like the Alaska Range, which includes Denali, the highest peak in North America, Alaska offers diverse landscapes of glaciers, fjords, and dense forests. Its pristine environment is home to wildlife such as bears, moose, and bald eagles, while its long summer days and harsh winters shape a unique, remote lifestyle. Rich in Native Alaskan cultures and gold rush history, Alaska also has vibrant fishing, oil, and tourism industries.
